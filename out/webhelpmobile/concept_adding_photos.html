Adding Photos

You can add photos to your project, stash, or profile pages.

There are several ways to add photos to your Ravelry pages, depending on the type of photo you want to add and where the photo is located.

  • Your Stash
    Keep track of your yarn inventory in the Stash section of your notebook.
  • Adding Photos from Flickr
    You can add photos to your project and stash pages from your Flickr account. Ravelry is optimized to work with Flickr.
  • Adding Photos from Photobucket
    You can add photos to your project or stash pages from your Photobucket account.
  • Adding Photos from the Web
    If you use a website other than Flickr or Photobucket to store your photos, you can still add them to your project or stash pages.
  • Adding Photos from Your Computer
    You can add photos to your stash or project pages directly from your computer.
