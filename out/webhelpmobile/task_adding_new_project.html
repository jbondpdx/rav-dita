Adding a New Project

Add your new projects to your Ravelry notebook.

Add new projects to the project page in your personal notebook to keep track of details, notes, and progress.

  1. Click on My Notebook.
  2. Click on Add Project.
  3. Give your project any name you like.
  4. Fill in the name of the pattern and the source of the pattern and click Continue.
  5. Complete your project information with size, yarn, tags, and other information you want to add.
    1. Optional: Click Use Stash Yarn if you want to link to yarn already listed in your stash.
  6. Click Save Changes.
