Adding Photos from Flickr

You can add photos to your project and stash pages from your Flickr account. Ravelry is optimized to work with Flickr.

Before you can add photos from Flickr, you will need to link your Flickr account to your Ravelry account.
Note: Your photos must be publicly viewable on Flickr to appear on Ravelry. Private photos or photos deleted from Flickr are automatically deleted from Ravelry.
To add photos from Flickr to your project and stash pages:
  1. From the project or stash page you want to add photos to, click Add Photos.
  2. Optional: From the dropdown menu, select the Flickr set containing your photos.
  3. Optional: In the tag field, add any photo tags you want to search for.
  4. Click Refresh to show the results of your photo search.
  5. Select the photo you want and drag it to the photo area.
