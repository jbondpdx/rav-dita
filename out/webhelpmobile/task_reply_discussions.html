Replying to Group Discussions

Join discussions to chat about your common interests.

Join the discussions in your groups to share ideas and get to know your fellow Ravelers better.

  1. From your group's page, click on Discussions.
  2. To reply to an existing discusssion, click the thread you want to post in.
  3. You can reply to the thread or you can reply to a specific post.
    • Click Reply to Thread to post a reply to the entire thread, rather than to one specific person's post.
    • Click Reply on a specific post to reply to that post. The original poster will be notified of your reply.
  4. Compose your post and click Post Reply.
