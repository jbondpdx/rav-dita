Linking Your Flickr Account to Ravelry

To add photos from Flickr, you will first need to link your Flickr account to your Ravelry account.

To link your Flickr account to Ravelry:

  1. From one of your saved projects or stash items, click Add Photos.
  2. Click on Link to Your Flickr Account.
  3. When the Flickr/Yahoo login page appears, login to your Flickr account.
  4. When asked if you will allow Ravelry to link to your Flickr account, click OK.
