Getting Started on Ravelry

Joining Groups

Join groups to meet others who share your interests.

Ravelry users have created thousands of interest groups, so you can find knitters and crocheters who share your interests, both fiber- and non-fiber-related.

  1. Click on Groups.
  2. Browse or search groups by location, interest, or tags.
    • Click on Browse by Location to find groups in various geographical locations.
    • Click on Search by Tags to view a cloud of tags for different groups.
    • Type a search term in the search field and click Search to look for specific groups.
  3. Once you have found a group you want to join, click Join Group on that group's page.
  • Replying to Group Discussions
    Join discussions to chat about your common interests.
  • Starting New Group Discussions
    Don't be shy! Start new discussions in your groups.
