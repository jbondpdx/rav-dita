Searching Patterns

Search the database for specific patterns.

If you know what you are looking for, search the database for a specific pattern.

  1. Click Patterns.
  2. In the search field, type a search term. Search terms can include tags, type of project, or the name of the pattern, designer, or publisher. Click Search.
  3. Optional: Select filters to narrow your results.
