Adding Photos

Adding Photos from Your Computer

You can add photos to your stash or project pages directly from your computer.

  1. From the project or stash page you want to add photos to, click Add Photos.
  2. Click Upload from Computer.
  3. Click Select File to Upload to browse for your photo.
  4. When you have selected the photo, click Upload Now. Your photo will automatically be resized.
