Your Queue

Your Queue is your list of planned projects.

You can keep a list of projects you plan to make in your Queue. You can add to your Queue from yarn or pattern pages. For each Queue item, you can include information about what yarn you plan to use, who the project is for, and whether you have a deadline for the project. You can organize and order your Queue by tag, yarn type, priority, or deadline.

  • Organizing Your Queue

  • Reordering Your Queue
    You can rearrange the order of your queue.

    Remove items you no longer want to make from your queue.
