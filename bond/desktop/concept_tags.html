Tags

You can use tags on Ravelry to help you sort and search for projects, yarns, discussion posts, and more.

Tags on Ravelry work like keywords for patterns, projects, yarns, discussion posts: pretty much anything on Ravelry can be tagged. With tagging, you can easily search or browse for related items.

Tags on Ravelry are separated by spaces, but no spaces are used within tags. Use tags for words or terms that represent something significant about the item you are tagging (like toeup, stockinette, or winterwhite)
