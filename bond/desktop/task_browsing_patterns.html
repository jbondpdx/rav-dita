Browsing Patterns

If you're not sure exactly what pattern you're looking for, browse through the database.

Browse our lists of tags, categories, and popular and recently added patterns in the Patterns tab. If you prefer more targeted browsing, use the Pattern Browser and Advanced Search to narrow your browsing to specific categories.

  1. Click on Patterns.
  2. Click on Pattern Browser & Advanced Search.
  3. Click the filter options to narrow your browsing results by craft, tag, category, yarn weight, yardage, difficulty, and other attributes. You can apply multiple filters to your results.
