Editing Your Profile

Tell other Ravelers more about yourself in your profile

  1. Click on your username to go to your profile.
  2. Click Edit My Profile.
  3. Set your privacy options.
  4. Fill in the URL for your blog or website. A thumbnail of your site will appear on your profile page.
  5. Complete your personal information with your location, birthday, favorite colors and curse words, and other information. Fill out as much or as little as you like.
  6. Click Add Social Sites to link your Facebook, Twitter, Yelp, or other social network accounts to your profile page. This helps people find you outside of Ravelry.
  7. Click Save Changes.
  • Adding Profile Photos
    Add a personal photo to your profile.
