Your Queue

Adding Projects to Your Queue

Keep track of the projects you want to make in your queue.

Add projects you want to make in the future to your queue. You can add projects to your queue from either yarn or pattern pages.
  1. From any yarn or pattern page, click Add to Queue.
  2. Complete the Pattern and Yarn fields.
  3. Optional: If you want to link to patterns or yarns in Ravelry's database, click the Link icon next to the field.
  4. Optional: Complete the fields to add tags, a project recipient, a deadline, or notes.
  5. Click Save.
