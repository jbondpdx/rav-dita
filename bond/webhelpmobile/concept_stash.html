Your Stash

Keep track of your yarn inventory in the Stash section of your notebook.

Concept definition.

  • Adding New Stash Yarn
    Add new yarns to your stash so you always know what you have on hand.
  • Assigning Stash to Projects
    If you're using your stash yarn for a project, assign your stash yarns to your project.
  • Deleting Stash
    Remove yarn from your stash to keep your inventory up-to-date.
