Getting Started on Ravelry

Welcome to Ravelry!

The best way to learn how to use Ravelry is to just jump in and start exploring. On Ravelry, you can track your projects and stash, find new patterns and yarns, and connect with other knitters, crocheters, and spinners. Some of the most common tasks new users want to try include:

  • Editing Your Profile
    Tell other Ravelers more about yourself in your profile
  • Finding Patterns
    Find patterns in Ravelry's pattern database.
  • Adding a New Project
    Add your new projects to your Ravelry notebook.
  • Joining Groups
    Join groups to meet others who share your interests.
