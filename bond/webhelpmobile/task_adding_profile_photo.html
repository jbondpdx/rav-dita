Adding Profile Photos

Add a personal photo to your profile.

Your profile picture or Ravatar represents you throughout Ravelry. It appears on your profile page, next to your projects, in your groups: anywhere you are active.
Remember: Ravatars may not contain nudity, profanity, or hate speech.
  1. Click on your username to go to your profile.
  2. Click Edit My Profile.
  3. Click Add Profile Pics.
  4. Note: Profile photos must be PNG or JPG format and must be uploaded from your computer.
    Click Browse to browse to the photo on your computer.
  5. Click Upload.
